About the Owner

Catherine Farley is the granddaughter of the original owner of Bebe's Bungalow, Florence "Bebe" Belay. She used to visit her grandmother at the cabin on Lake Vermilion every summer when she was a kid growing up in Eau Claire, WI. She always enjoyed the endless activities available up north and, above all, spending time with family. Her favorite activity to do with Bebe was go fishing for walleye. Catherine now owns the cabin after Bebe passed in 2004 and enjoys living up north year round, surrounded by friends, family, and the beautiful outdoors.
