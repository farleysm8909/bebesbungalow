About the Cabin

Bebe's Bungalow has been in the family ever since Florence "Bebe" Belay purchased it in 1975. Since then, it has become a place for family and friends to gather and spend quality time together while enjoying all that Lake Vermilion has to offer.

The following video was filmed and edited by the very talented Trey Fortner. It offers a glimpse as to what life is like at the cabin during the summer—grilling, hanging out by the fire, fireworks over the lake, watersports, and spending time with loved ones.

About the Owner

Catherine Farley is the granddaughter of the original owner of Bebe's Bungalow, Florence "Bebe" Belay. Catherine used to visit her grandmother at the cabin on Lake Vermilion every summer when she was a kid growing up in Eau Claire, WI. She always enjoyed the endless activities available up north and, above all, spending time with family. Her favorite activity with Bebe was fishing for walleye. Catherine now owns the cabin after Bebe passed in 2004 and enjoys living up north year round, surrounded by friends, family, and the beautiful outdoors.
